#Ingredients
Apple Filling
5–6 apples (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix)
2 tbsp sugar (use brown or white — your call)
1 tbsp lemon juice (optional but nice)
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp nutmeg (optional)
1 tbsp flour or cornstarch (thickens the juices)
Crisp Topping
¾ cup old-fashioned oats
½ cup all-purpose flour
½ cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
¼ tsp salt
6 tbsp cold butter, cut into small cubes
#Instructions
Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
Prep apples
Peel, core, and slice apples.
Toss them in a bowl with sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour.
Spread evenly in a greased baking dish (8×8 or similar).
Make the topping
Mix oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
Cut in the cold butter using fingers or a fork until crumbly.
Assemble
Sprinkle topping evenly over apples.
Bake
Bake uncovered for 40–45 minutes until golden brown and bubbly.
Cool slightly
Let sit for ~10 minutes so it sets.
